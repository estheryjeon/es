FAQs

Will the wedding be indoors or outdoors?

The ceremony will be outdoors and the reception will be indoors. In case of inclement weather, both will be held indoors.

Is there a recommended hotel?

Yes! We're finalizing a hotel block. We'll update everyone with it soon!

Can I bring a date?

Please refer to your invitation for other guests. We want to keep this intimate and won't be able to accomodate additional guests. If you have any questions, feel free to contact Esther or Soonho.

What time should I arrive?

The ceremony will start promptly at 4:30pm. Please arrive early for parking and finding seats.

What will the weather be like?

The temperature is typically in the 60s in April. It will likely get chillier as the sun sets. We recommend bringing a light jacket.

How bad is the Bay Bridge traffic?

It’s pretty bad. We recommend budgeting 1 hour if you’re driving from San Francisco.

What is the parking situation?

There is a parking lot right in front of the venue for about 40 cars. If that lot is full, there is plenty of street parking.

I have more questions! Who should I ask?

Contact either Soonho or Esther.
